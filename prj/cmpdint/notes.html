Compounding Interest Notes

This app can do various computations related to compounding interest. For example,

  • compute an amortization table based on a principal value, interest rate, payment amount, and payment period,
  • compute a growth table based on a principal value, interest rate, compounding period, and growth period,
  • determine the interest rate required for a principal of X to grow to a total amount of Y
  • given target amount, interest rate, and time for growth, determine the starting amount required,
  • etc...
