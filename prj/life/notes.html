Conway's Life

Conway's Life processes determinsitic automata. Each pixel is "alive" or "dead". If it's alive, it is set to black, otherwise white. At each iteration, a pixel comes to life or stays alive if it has three living neighbors. It stays alive if it has two. Otherwise, it dies.
